nendran
Banana

Nourished by the perennial waters of the Bhavani River, our Nendran bananas are the pinnacle of Southern Indian agriculture. Cultivated in the fertile plains of Gobichettipalayam, these "Golden Bananas" are distinguished by their large size, thick skin, and distinct angular shape. Whether used for gourmet cooking, making the world-famous "Nendran Chips," or eaten ripe for its high probiotic value, our Gobi-grown Nendran is the preferred choice for the European and Middle Eastern markets.

Purity & Source: Ethically Grown & Traceable

  • Sourced from the Bhavani-LBP canal region. We follow "Good Agricultural Practices" (GAP) to ensure the fruits are free from chemical residues. Our Nendran is known for its high dry-matter content, making it superior for processing.

Quality & Grade: Grade ‘A’ Export Quality

  • Length: 20 cm – 25 cm+ per finger.
  • Shape: Characteristically long, slightly curved.
  • Texture: Firm and starchy when green; soft and honey-sweet when fully ripe.

Packaging Detail

  • Net Weight: 2kg ,5kg ,8kg,15kg Carton
  • Hand Arrangement:Packed in "u-shape" or "row" patterns to prevent neck-breaking.
  • Box: 5-ply Bottom / 3-ply Top Strong Carton

State of Origin: Gobi, Tamil Nadu (India)

  • The Red Loamy Soil of the Erode district gives our Nendran a unique nutritional profile, specifically higher levels of Vitamin A and Potassium compared to those grown in sandy soils.
